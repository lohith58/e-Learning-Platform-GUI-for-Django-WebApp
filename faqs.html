How to report website related issue?

For any issues, please drop an email to lohith58malasaniii@gmail.com with the complete details.


I have issues with Login, what should I do?

You can click on Forgot Password link to reset your password from the login screen. We will send the new password to your registered email address. Try to login with the new password.


How to create an account?

You need to create an account before taking a course. Click on Menu icon present on the top left of the Store Screen. From the opened menu,Click on Sign Up. Enter your Email Id & Password to register for an account.


How to take a Course?

After you have purchased the Course or used the access code of the course, the course would be added to your My Course Section. Click on the Course image to start the course.


Do you offer refunds?

We don't offer any refund currently because the courses are digital , once you purchased can not be returned.


What if I have doubts during the lecture?

Each video lecture has discussion box for that particular lecture related doubts. You can commit your doubts there and Instructor will answer them within as early as possible .
